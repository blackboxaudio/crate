Crate includes a built-in audio player for previewing tracks. Use it to audition music without leaving the application.

## Player Location

The player appears at the bottom of the Crate window. It shows:

- **Track artwork** - Album cover of the current track
- **Track info** - Title and artist
- **Progress bar** - Current position and total duration
- **Controls** - Play, pause, stop

## Playing a Track

### Double-Click

The simplest way to play a track:

1. Find the track in the library or a playlist
2. Double-click the track row
3. Playback starts immediately

### Context Menu

1. Right-click on a track
2. Select **Play**

## Player Controls

### Play/Pause

Toggle playback with:
- Click the **Play/Pause** button in the player
- Press **Space** on your keyboard (when not in a text field)

### Stop

Click the **Stop** button to halt playback and clear the current track.

### Seek

To jump to a different position in the track:
- **Click** anywhere on the progress bar to jump to that position
- **Drag** the playhead to scrub through the track

### Volume

Adjust playback volume with the volume slider in the player.

## Now Playing Display

When a track is playing, the player shows:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Artwork | Album cover (if available) |
| Title | Track name |
| Artist | Performer |
| Progress | Current time / Total time |

The currently playing track is also highlighted in the track list.

## Audio Device Selection

Choose which audio output device Crate uses for playback:

1. Open **Settings** (`Cmd+,` / `Ctrl+,`)
2. Go to the **Sound** tab
3. Select your preferred output device
4. Close settings

Changes take effect for the next track played.

### Available Devices

Crate detects:
- Built-in speakers/headphones
- USB audio interfaces
- Bluetooth audio devices
- Virtual audio devices

If a device is disconnected during playback, audio will stop. Reconnect the device and resume playback.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Action | Shortcut |
|--------|----------|

More playback shortcuts may be added in future updates.

## Tips

### Quick Previews

Use the player for quick auditions:
1. Browse your library
2. Double-click to preview
3. Move to the next track when ready

### Device Setup for DJing

If using Crate alongside DJ software:
1. Route Crate to a separate output (e.g., headphones)
2. Keep your main output for DJ software
3. Preview tracks in Crate without affecting your mix

### Troubleshooting

**No sound?**
- Check the volume slider in the player
- Verify the correct output device is selected in Settings
- Ensure your system volume is up
- Try selecting a different audio device

**Audio stutters or crackles?**
- Close other audio applications
- Try a different audio device
- Restart Crate
